
# 🔑 Key to Escape


Key to Escape is a dark-themed 3D maze survival game where players must explore dangerous environments, avoid traps, collect keys, and escape before losing all their lives. The game combines exploration, puzzle-solving, and survival mechanics to create a tense and engaging gameplay experience.

--

Playable Link :
https://mohd-sheharyar.itch.io/key-to-escape
   
# 🎮 Game Overview

In Key to Escape, players are placed inside a mysterious maze filled with hidden dangers and challenging pathways. The objective is to locate different keys scattered throughout the map while avoiding traps and surviving environmental hazards.

The game focuses on:

* Maze exploration
* Survival gameplay
* Trap avoidance
* Key collection mechanics
* Health and life management
* Dark atmospheric environment

---

# ✨ Features

## 🌑 Dark Maze Environment

* Atmospheric dark-themed level design
* Immersive maze exploration
* Suspense-driven gameplay experience

## 🗝️ Key Collection System

Players must collect multiple types of keys to unlock the final escape route.

Includes:

* Multiple collectible keys
* Exploration-based progression
* Objective tracking system

## ⚠️ Trap System

The maze contains dangerous traps that reduce player health and increase difficulty.

Examples:

* Damage zones
* Hidden obstacles
* Environmental hazards

## ❤️ Health and Life System

* Players start with 3 lives
* Health pickups are placed throughout the map
* Strategic survival mechanics

## 🎯 Challenging Gameplay

* Increasing difficulty during progression
* Exploration and memory-based navigation
* Risk and reward gameplay structure
